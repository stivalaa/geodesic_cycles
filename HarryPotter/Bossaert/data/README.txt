Description
A sequence of networks representing narrative support among characters in the first six Harry Potter books. Nodes are characters, and a directed edge (i,j) indicates that character i gives peer support (emotional support, instructional help, certain information help, or praise) to character j. Metadata includes character name, school year, gender, and wizarding house.
